Researching literature

In the lecture I mentioned using the ACM Digital Library and the IEEE Explore Library . You can get full access to articles in the ACM Digital Library, at least if you log in from departmental machines (I'm not sure what happens beyond the department). Unfortunately, for the IEEE we only appear to have a licence which gives us access to abstracts of most papers. For full content, you may need to order items through Inter-Library loan at the Library, or find out whether your supervisor is a member of IEEE (I'm not, so I'm afraid I can't help).
