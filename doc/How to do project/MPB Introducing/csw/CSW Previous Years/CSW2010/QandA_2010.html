LAST DAY FOR QUESTIONS: 17 JANUARY 2011

These are questions and answers about the CSW assessment for 2010-11.

Questions must be e-mailed to pirs@cs.york.ac.uk. Some of questions will be answered on this page.

Questions and their answers are given in reverse order of their answering. The dates are the dates that the answer is posted, not that when the question is asked. Questions will be made anonymous when transcribed here.

Questions are emphasised, answers not:

/Aut/5/Fri

  • When choosing a subject area, the assesment guidelines say I should select from research groups of the department. Does this mean that I cant review papers that isnt researched at York, for example on malware detection?

You are not restricted to publications by York people. The restriction on subject area is not intended to be very strict, but I do want to prevent people doing a lit review on 'What I did in my summer holidays' or some other esoteric subject unrelated to CS. The restriction to 'York research group subject areas' is simply meant to be an easy way to define 'sensible bits of Computer Science'.

 

Stefano Pirandola
