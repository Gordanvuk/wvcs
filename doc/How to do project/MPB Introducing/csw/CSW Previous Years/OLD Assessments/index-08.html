Schedule for Presentations

The CSW presentations take place in week 4, Spring term: the sessions are on 29 and 31 January.

  • Allocation to slots is now avaiable. Scheduling is quite tight, so please arrive promptly for your session.
  • You should attend the whole of your allocated session. The order of presentation will be the order in which names are listed, unless anyone has a pressing need to change the order.
  • If you cannot attend your allocated session, please email me urgently, and no later than Wednesday week 3, stating why you cannot make the allocated session, and which other sessions you are available for.
  • Details of the rooms in which presentations are taking place can be found on the University web site, http://www.york.ac.uk/admin/estates/roombookings/: note that ds is the Harold Fairhair building, the portakabin behind Langwith; v/c is the new Vanburgh building housing LFA etc.
  • Presentations will be assessed by Fiona Polack, and one demonstrator; the allocated demonstrators are Kester Clegg and Matthew Grum.
